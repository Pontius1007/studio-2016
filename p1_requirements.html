Our client is a former musician turned producer, Peter Hansen, that now runs his own music studio. As a former artist, Peter has high demands when it comes to creativity and is expecting a website with a nice design and artistic layout. After some brainstorming via skype, we feel that we have achieved a good connection with Peter and a mutual understanding of what we all want the website to be.

Purpose and goals

To promote the studio, and give information about what the studio to potential customers.

It is important to make the webpage look as professional as possible to give a good impression to people visiting the page, due to the high competition in the buisness.

Target audience

The target audience is anyone interrested in recording something in the studio, getting a song produced produced, or someone interreseted any other thing the studio can offer. Other things might be studio lessons or borrowing equipment etc.

This would be anyone from music bands, solo artist, russ wanting a song or music students.

Preferences

We have in dialogue with the client specified a few preferences regarding the design and layout of the website we would like to implement:

  • The same color scheme relevant to the organization's logo used throughout the whole website.
  • A layout which is easy to understand and navigate which allows the user to find any information he might be interested in easily and quickly.
  • If possible, the client would like the user to have the option to toggle between a Norwegian and English version of the website.
  • We would like the website to be responsive, quickly loadable and convenient for the user to navigate across a variety of platforms. We must therefore be concious about the design decisions we make and how we implement them to ensure this.

Content and structure

An overview of the website's content and structure, and what is included in each page.

    Studio

  • Gives the user short information and some pictures of the studio

    • Who are we?

  • Includes a short introduction of the organization and how and when it was formed. It also mentions past achievements and future goals. Here you find contact info as well.

    • Producers

  • Some information about each producer in separate pages. Here you find a box for sending e-mail.

    • Lessons

  • Information about the different lessons, what we can offer and what you can expect from the instuctor.

    • Price

  • A table of the different prices for the lessons, borrowing equipment and record in the studio.

Hosting

The website will be hosted on the NTNU server.
