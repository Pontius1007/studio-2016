3. Purpose and goal

The website’s purpose is to spread and educate the public about the new studio in Trondheim, Northern Wave. In addition, to inform the audience about news and information regarding the studio, some focus will be given to the different producers. Each producer will have their own page to promote business and bring in costumers.

Other purposes would be to create revenue through the promotation of the Studio as well as bring in clientell.

4. Navigation Structure

The web site navigation is mostly based on a simple but effective layout, where you can access all websites on the page from every other page by the use of dropdown menues and a navigation bar. The main pages consists of index.html, information.html, studio.html, producers.html, lessons.html, prices.html and who-are-we.html

The sites not included, that has to be accessed either by a dropdown meny or through other sites are equipment.html, accessable through studio.html, and the three producers pages. These can be reached by entering the producer.html page. THese pages can visit all other pages.

The structure is described in the directed graph below. The "*" behind studio.html and producers.html means there will be several pages in the same category with the same navigation structure. This is done to keep the navigation bar clean, and still keep the user a maximum of three clicks away from the destination at any given time.

The clique structure is chosen to give the user the freedom they deserve.

5. Page Layout and Appearance

The canvas size used in the mockups is 860 px content width. The header and footer will take up the whole body and will not be contained within the 860px wrapper used for text and pictures. The navigation bar will be at most 860px wide, but will be responsive to any changes in the width.

The logo will be placed in the top left corner of the header, and the social media bar will be placed to the right. This will move closer to each other if the width of the page changes.

On mobile/pads, the responsive design will resize the elements to take 100% of the width, so thus navigation bar togheter with the header and footer might have to be presented in another way.

The background will not be white, but we are using a picture as background layout. Will be explained in detail below.

6. Content

The content uses 9 unique pages with a few additional sub-pages. Some pages utilize a similar layout.

Front Page

The front page will be the first page the user visits. It will contain a picture spanning across the screen and will promptly inform the user that it is the website of a studio.

The homepage will fulfil the goal of informing the audience about the studios design and equipment. By the use of JavaScript we will rotate the picture shown on the frontpage to highlight different parts of the studio.

Main goal of the front page is to give a stylish impression to the studios future costumers.

About

The “About Us” page contains general information about the organization. This includes a description of early history, a summary of achievements and a short description of what the organization aims to achieve. Additionally the page will have links to official social media.

This page exists to introduce the organization and try to make visitors to empathize with the different teams within. The page will achieve this by including great moments from the past as well as including visitor in on their aspirations. Links to social media is included to hook the players in to a form of community.

Content will be in the centre of the page with a navigation bar on the right side. The navigation bar contains subsections of the page; the transition between subpages will be done with javascript.

Pictures relating to the organization will be added to break up the text and to make the page easier to digest. The pictures will be small in size to optimize page loading.

Team

The team/games page will show the user what games the team currently has players in, as well as the players for each of the games. It has it's own navigation bar under the one that is shared between the pages, containing all the games. When you click one of the games, all the players playing this game will be displayed below. All the player images will send you to that player's page. The player images is lined up horizontally.

We took inspiration from TeamLiquid's layout, and borrowed placeholder pictures from their page found here

Player

The player page, is opened when the user selects a player on the teams/members –page. There is one page for each individual team member.

This page contains, the player’s nickname, an image of the player, his/her real name, age, country of origin, and a witty quote. It also contains links to the team member’s various social media pages, as well as a donate button. There will be a link the player’s stream if he/she has one.

The purpose of this page is to provide basic information about who this team member is, with focus on gaming achievements and to induce an interest in learning more. It should also produce some extra income to the player through donations. Users, who wish to know more, can then easily find more information through the social media links.

Sponsors

The sponsors page will inform the users who is sponsoring the team. It also contains a little bit of information about the sponsors, and where to learn more about them.

Each sponsor will have their picture placed to the left on the screen, with a text box containing information about them to the right. The box on the right will also contain some method of finding out more about the sponsor.

Shop

This page opens when the user presses the shop link in the navigation bar.

The page contains images of various merchandise as well as an order button. Clicking on a product displays a drop-down menu where the user can chose how many they wish to order of that item. Hovering over a product displays a box with additional product information. There is also an order button, which links to the order form.

The purpose is to quickly display the organizations merchandise, and entice the user to order some of it.

Contact

The “Contact Us” page will include information about how to get in touch with the organization. It will have the mail and phone number to get in touch with us. We will limit the use of text on this page as the focus is on the information present. By pressing the mail button you will be redirected to a page where you can write a mail to us.
