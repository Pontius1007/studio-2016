3. Purpose and goal

The website’s purpose is to spread and educate the public about the new studio in Trondheim, Northern Wave. In addition, to inform the audience about news and information regarding the studio, some focus will be given to the different producers. Each producer will have their own page to promote business and bring in costumers.

Other purposes would be to create revenue through the promotation of the Studio as well as bring in clientell.

4. Navigation Structure

The web site navigation is mostly based on a simple but effective layout, where you can access all websites on the page from every other page by the use of dropdown menues and a navigation bar. The main pages consists of index.html, information.html, studio.html, producers.html, lessons.html, prices.html and who-are-we.html

The sites not included, that has to be accessed either by a dropdown meny or through other sites are equipment.html, accessable through studio.html, and the three producers pages. These can be reached by entering the producer.html page. THese pages can visit all other pages.

The structure is described in the directed graph below. The "*" behind studio.html and producers.html means there will be several pages in the same category with the same navigation structure. This is done to keep the navigation bar clean, and still keep the user a maximum of three clicks away from the destination at any given time.

The clique structure is chosen to give the user the freedom they deserve.

5. Page Layout and Appearance

The canvas size used in the mockups is 860 px content width. The header and footer will take up the whole body and will not be contained within the 860px wrapper used for text and pictures. The navigation bar will be at most 860px wide, but will be responsive to any changes in the width.

The logo will be placed in the top left corner of the header, and the social media bar will be placed to the right. This will move closer to each other if the width of the page changes.

On mobile/pads, the responsive design will resize the elements to take 100% of the width, so thus navigation bar togheter with the header and footer might have to be presented in another way.

The background will not be white, but we are using a picture as background layout. Will be explained in detail below.

6. Content

The content uses 9 unique pages with a few additional sub-pages. Some pages utilize a similar layout.

Front Page

The front page will be the first page the user visits. It will contain a picture spanning across the screen and will promptly inform the user that it is the website of a studio.

The homepage will fulfil the goal of informing the audience about the studios design and equipment. By the use of JavaScript we will rotate the picture shown on the frontpage to highlight different parts of the studio.

Main goal of the front page is to give a stylish impression to the studios future costumers.

Information

The “Information” page contains general information about the studio. This includes a description of early history, information about upcoming events and other stories related to artists and/or songs produced by the studio.

This page exists to introduce the studio to potential costumers and enlighten them about the archievements done by the studio. The page will achieve this by including great moments from the past as well as including their future aspirations.

Content will be in the centre of the page.

Pictures relating to the studio will be added to break up the text and to make the page easier to digest. The pictures will be small in size to optimize page loading and wide enough to cover the 860px body.

Studio

The Studio page will contain some information about what we can offer our customers. Some history about the studio and other interesting facts about the studio. A linked picture to the equipment list and a video tour of the studio in the bottom of the page.

The purpose of this page is to provide some information about the studio and the equipment so the costumer know what he can expect.

Who are We

The Who are We page will contain a header image of the different people working in the studio and a description of the workplace itself.

The purpose of this page is to provide basic information about who works and makes the studio go around. By the help of in depth information the costumer will gain trust, and if interested, contact Northern Wave by clicking the link.

Producers

The producer page will contain a picture of each producers avaliable for hire. Next to the picture some information will be present and inform the costumer about what services the producer provides. Clicking on either the picture or the text will redirect the user to the producers own page.

Each producer will have their picture placed to the left on the screen, with a text box containing information about them to the right. The box on the right will also contain some method of finding out more about the producer.

Lessons

The page contains 4 containers each describing different lessons a potential costumer can be interested in. Information about each product will be displayed and the costumer can click a link to either rent producers for the task or to buy lessons.

The purpose is to quickly display the studios merchandise, and entice the user to order some of it.

Producer

The “Producer” page will include information about how to get in touch with the producer in question. It will inform about the producers merits, skills and motivation. On the right a form can be filled out to contact the producer with an offer for some work. There will be a total of 3 producer pages and each producer has their own skills compared to the rest.

The purpose of this page is to promote each producer and gain revenue for the studio.

Price

The price page will have a table containing the different prices for the different services provided by the stuido. The price for a producer will not be listed there, but in the producer page.

The purpose of this page is to promote the different products that the studio sells and their prices.

Equipment

This page will list all the equipment avaliable to rent/use in the studio.

The purpose of this page is to promote the different equipment in the studio.
