Our client is a former musician turned producer, Peter Hansen, that now runs his own music studio. As a former artist, Peter has high demands when it comes to creativity and is expecting a website with a nice design and artistic layout. After some brainstorming via skype, we feel that we have achieved a good connection with Peter and a mutual understanding of what we all want the website to be.

Purpose and goals

To inform the audience about news and information regarding the organization and team, upcoming events where the organization will be present, and promote its players and endeavors as well as sponsors.

Other purposes would be to create revenue through the online shop, and provide a channel for communication.

Target audience

The audience is

Main audience is the fans and followers of the team, as well as other individuals with a general interest in the esports scene, the organization and its player’s endeavors.

It is important to mention there could be individuals or organizations with business inquiries who potentially look for information outside of the main audience’ interests, and to keep these interests in mind when developing the website’s content and design.

It is NOT a platform for potential players who wish to tryout for the team.

Preferences

We have in dialogue with the client specificied a few preferences regarding the design and layout of the website we would like to implement:

  • The same color scheme relevant to the organization's logo used throughout the whole website.
  • A layout which is easy to understand and navigate which allows the user to find any information he might be interested in easily and quickly.
  • If possible, the client would like the user to have the option to toggle between a Norwegian and English version of the website.
  • We would like the website to be responsive, quickly loadable and convenient for the user to navigate across a variety of platforms. We must therefore be concious about the design decisions we make and how we implement them to ensure this.

Content and structure

An overview of the website's content and structure, and what is included in each page.

    News

  • Gives the user information about upcoming events, changes in management or roster, tournament results and other relevant information regarding the scene, organization, team or its endeavours

    • About

  • Includes a short introduction of the organization and how and when it was formed. It also mentions past achievements and future goals.

    • Sponsors

  • Contains a list of all the organization's sponsors and a short description with logo and links to their respective sites and other social media platforms.

    • Contact

  • All available methods for contacting the organization

    • Teams

  • Gives an overview of all the teams within the organization. Each team also includes a subpage with information about the player roster and for each individual player.

    • Shop

  • A shop including team merchandise/products that are sold through the organization.

Hosting

The website will be hosted on the NTNU server.

List of Requirements

    Logo

  • The organization’s logo must be visible from all parts of the website

    • Navigation

  • A navigation bar that is visible from all parts of the website
  • A clear way to see in which part of the website the user is currently located
  • The user must be able to access every part of the website within 3 clicks
  • A site-map with links to all different pages of the website

    • News page

  • News
  • List of upcoming events

    • About page

  • Short info paragraph(s) about the organization
  • List of past achievements
  • Plans and goals for the future

    • Team page

  • List of players categorized under their respective games where you can access the invidual players’ own subpage
  • Each individual player has their own subpage with relevant info

    • Sponsor page

  • List of every sponsor, their logo and links to their respective sites

    • Contact page

  • Links to social media
  • Email contact information

    • Shop(optional)

  • List of organization merchandise for sale, and a way to select and order items.
