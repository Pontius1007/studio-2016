Skog recording their first album at Norhern Wave.

The band Skog is recording their first album "Waves" october 16th. The band consists of Sondre Skorgen on vocals, Vegard Johansen on drums, Ole Nogva on bass, Oliver Baily on trumpet and Karina Sundby on backing vocals. They have becomn known in the music community in trondheim with their unique style of playing, and we are very exited to get to produce their first album here at Northern Wave. It will be released on spotify as soon as we have prefected it with our pimp equipment and skills!
